VIT Pune Societies Showcase

IEEE VIT Pune Student Branch

A technical hub that organizes hackathons, coding competitions, inter/intra college contests, guest lectures, and tech talks to foster innovation and learning.

Viculp – The Theatre Club

A passionate group of theatre enthusiasts representing VIT Pune in prestigious competitions like Firodiya Karandak and Purushottam Karandak with powerful stage performances.

NSS VIT Pune

Dedicated to social service, NSS students actively engage in village development, awareness drives, blood donation camps, and other noble causes that benefit society.

Velocee Racing India – Formula Student Club

Prestigious club from the Mechanical department, winners of Formula Bharat in Combustion category, now developing EVs with cutting-edge tech and engineering brilliance.

The Robotics Forum

Official robotics team of VIT Pune, competing nationally with top ranks such as AIR 5 in DD National Robocon at IIT Delhi. A hub for passionate innovators and engineers.
